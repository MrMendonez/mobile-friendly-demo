Big Hero 6

Big Hero 6 was the 2014 Disney animated hit that countless awards for its rich story, developed characters, and adult concepts weaved into a family-friendly movie, including Best Animated Feature in the 87th Academy Awards. This webpage serves to explain why adults and children alike, including me, were fascinated by this film.
